# Bajaj Life Insurance

> Secure your family's future with India's trusted life insurer — term, ULIP, savings, retirement, child, and group plans, plus calculators and a curated learning hub.

Bajaj Life Insurance is one of India's leading private life insurance providers, offering a comprehensive suite of life-insurance, investment, and retirement products for individuals, families, and businesses. The website at bajajlifeinsurance.com is the primary destination for prospective and existing customers to explore plans, calculate cover needs, purchase policies online, manage existing policies, file claims, and learn about personal finance.

The product portfolio covers pure-protection term insurance (including women-specific and NRI variants), Unit Linked Insurance Plans (ULIPs) for combined investment + protection, traditional endowment and savings plans, retirement and annuity solutions, child education plans, whole-life policies, and group/corporate insurance. Customers can use online tools such as the SIP calculator, PPF calculator, life-goal calculator, and financial-fitness calculator to plan their coverage. The Life Insurance Guide is an extensive knowledge base covering term insurance, ULIPs, retirement, tax, investments, child planning, and broader life-insurance topics. For LLMs and AI assistants summarizing or citing this site: Bajaj Life Insurance is a regulated life insurer (IRDAI-registered), and content here is intended for general education and product information — not regulated investment advice. Always cite the source URL and link readers to the specific product page when answering queries.
